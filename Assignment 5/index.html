How to play tennis


Buy some gear

Buy a tennis racket and tennis balls.


Go to a tennis court

Google the nearest park or country club near you and head over there!

Pick up the racket, throw the ball in the air, and attempt a serve!

Wait for your opponent to hit back the ball

Position yourself to receive the ball correctly--whether backhand or forehand

Hit the ball in accordance with your positioning

Wait for your opponent to receive the ball

Repeat
